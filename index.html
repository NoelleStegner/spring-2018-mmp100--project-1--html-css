Hideo Kojima

Hideo Kojima from Hideo Kojima's Twitter Profile Picture

Hideo Kojima is a legendary video game producer considered the Steven Spielberg of gaming and has created some of most intricate well known games of the past two decades. He's known for titles Metal Gear Solid, PT Silent Hills, The Evil Within, Death Stranding and Snatchers. He used to be one of the directors of Komani video game productions but has now recently drifted off to his own company by the name of Kojima Productions.
